
# Word Cup (Format) Trivia

- 1934, 1938  -- replays  
- 1950        -- no final! (no semi-finals, no quarter-finals - only final round!)
- 1954, 1958  -- group play-offs (deciders)

- 1970 -- start of subs(titions) in 1970 Mexico
     -  before that, no substitutions were allowed at all—if a player got injured, the team just had to continue with fewer players


- 1978 -- start of penalty shoot-outs in 1978 Argentina
      - first actual shoot-out in 1982 Spain
        - ▪ Semi-final - West Germany v France   3-3 a.e.t., 5-4 pen.


- 1998 (+2002) -- start of sudden death golden goal  (a.e.t./g.g.) in 1998 France (and 2002 South Korea & Japan) 
  - 1998 France 
     -  ▪ Round of 16 - France v Paraguay  1-0 a.e.t./g.g.
  - 2002 South Korea & Japan
    -  ▪ Round of 16 - Sweden v Senegal                1-2 a.e.t./g.g.
    -                  South Korea v Italy             2-1 a.e.t./g.g.
    -  ▪ Quarter-final Senegal v Turkey                0-1 a.e.t./g.g.
  

- 1982 (-1994)  -- start of 24 teams (6x4 groups A,B,C,D,E,F) in 1982 Spain (to 1994 USA)
- 1998 (-2022)  -- start of 32 teams (8x4 groups A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H) in 1998 France (to 2022 Qatar)
- 2026 (-present) -- start of 48 teams (12x4 groups A,B,C,D,E,F,G,H,I,J,K,L) in 2026 Canada/USA/Mexico
