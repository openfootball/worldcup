= World Cup 1986        # in Mexico, 31 May-29 June


### --- Knockout stage

▪ Round of 16

15 June   Mexico       2-0    Bulgaria   @ Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
15 June   Soviet Union  3-4 a.e.t.  (2-2)   Belgium  @ Estadio Nou Camp, León
16 June   Argentina    1-0    Uruguay  @ Estadio Cuauhtémoc, Puebla
16 June   Brazil       4-0    Poland     @ Estadio Jalisco, Guadalajara
17 June   Italy      0-2    France       @ Estadio Olímpico Universitario, Mexico City

18 June   England    3-0    Paraguay   @ Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
18 June   Denmark    1-5    Spain      @ Estadio La Corregidora, Querétaro


▪ Quarter-finals

21 June    Brazil   1-1 a.e.t. (1-1), 3-4 pen.  France          @ Estadio Jalisco, Guadalajara
21 June    West Germany  0-0 a.e.t. (0-0), 4-1 pen.  Mexico   @ Estadio Universitario, Monterrey
22 June    Spain    1-1 a.e.t. (1-1), 4-5 pen.    Belgium      @ Estadio Cuauhtémoc, Puebla
22 June    Argentina    2-1    England              @ Estadio Azteca, Mexico City

▪ Semi-finals

25 June    Argentina    2-0    Belgium      @ Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
25 June    France       0-2    West Germany     @ Estadio Jalisco, Guadalajara

▪ Third place match

28 June    France   4-2 a.e.t. (2-2)   Belgium      @ Estadio Cuauhtémoc, Puebla

▪ Final

29 June    Argentina    3-2    West Germany  @ Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
